Responsible AI

Promoting responsible use of artificial intelligence (AI) is crucial for ensuring ethical and sustainable technological advancements. Responsible AI entails developing and deploying AI systems that are transparent, fair, and respect privacy and human rights. It involves implementing robust guidelines to prevent biases, ensuring data security, and fostering accountability in AI decision-making processes. Encouraging interdisciplinary collaboration among technologists, ethicists, and policymakers is vital to address the multifaceted challenges AI presents
